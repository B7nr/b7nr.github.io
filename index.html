About Me

My name is Ben Rehfeld and I am seventeen years old. I am a junior at St. John XXIII College Preparatory. This is my second year teaching swim lessons. I have been swimming for ten years. Nine of those ten years have swam competitively for Katy Aquatics. Not only have I swam for Katy Aquatics, but also for my high school swim team. This year I became the State Champion in the 200 free for TAPPS Division II.

Location of Lesson

I drive to the pool of your choice, whether it is a public pool or a private pool.

Pricing

The starting price of each lesson is fifteen dollars for a thirty minute lesson. The price may increase depending on travel time. Specific price will be discussed after the evaluation lesson.

Lessons

I offer swim lessons to swimmers of all skill levels and ages. The lessons can range from learning how swim to learning how to improve technique and speed of all four strokes.
